Powerful Processor

Powerful processor in a laptop is important for smooth performance and multitasking. Apple laptops, like the MacBook Pro, are known for their powerful processors.

High-resolution Display

High-resolution displays on laptops make everything look incredibly sharp and vibrant. Apple laptops, like the MacBook Pro, feature Retina displays, which have a pixel density that's so high, you won't be able to spot individual pixels.

Long Battery Life

Having a long battery life on a laptop is super important, especially if you're constantly on the go. Apple laptops, like the MacBook Air, are known for their impressive battery life. The latest models can last up to 12 hours on a single charge.

Memory storage

Memory or RAM is the space devoted to running your operating system and applications. To run multiple applications at once a Mac requires more memory. The more demanding those applications are, the more RAM you will be using.
